Our Second workshop series focuses on Mental Health and Wellbeing because businesses with unhealthy cultures will always struggle to adopt the best social, environmental and ethical practices.

MENTAL HEALTH AND WELLBEING COURSE

Our next workshop series focuses on the health and wellbeing of the company, because businesses need to focus on their workers in order to adopt the best social, environmental and ethical practices.


Just as organizations need to focus on the culture of their company in order to succeed, they also need to make sure the mental health and wellbeing of their employees.


The mental health and wellbeing of your employees is a very important thing to your business, but it can be hard to measure and understand, which is partially why this workshop is so important.

If the mental health and wellbeing of your employees is not in good shape, your company's output will also not be as good as it could be.


Given that this issue is so important, it makes it incredibly important to stay on top of this issue and keep the mental health and wellbeing of your employees strong.


This workshop will go into where your employees currently see the mental health and wellbeing support of your company is, and where you can improve.

WHO IS THE COURSE FOR?
  • A BUSINESS OWNER or director
  • A MANAGER who works closely with any operational or ethics functions
  • AN EMPLOYEE with any responsibility for HR, culture, operations ethics, strategy
  • ANY OTHER EMPLOYEE! Because culture is for, about and lived by everyone in the organisation, we support every business in including as many employees as possible into the culture work we'll undertake together.
COURSE BENEFITS
  • Build a healthy and supportive environment for your employees
  • Go beyond job descriptions and unlock the true potential of your people
  • Gain actionable insights that can be applied to the day to day running of your business
  • Create a bespoke roadmap that will allow your business to grow without sacrificing employee wellbeing, ethics or integrity
  • Receive useful tools and resources on how to be a responsible business
  • Join a dynamic peer support network that navigates future challenges together
  • Create live case studies reflecting your culture and your journey, to share with your stakeholders
